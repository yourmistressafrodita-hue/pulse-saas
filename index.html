Why we built PULSE

Founded in 2022, PULSE was born from a simple frustration: why is it so hard to get actionable insights from your own data? Our founders — former data engineers at leading tech companies — set out to build the analytics platform they always wished existed.

We've spent years in the trenches wiring together data pipelines, maintaining fragile dashboards, and waiting hours for queries to run. We knew there had to be a better way — so we built it.

Today, PULSE serves 2,500+ companies across 60 countries, from scrappy startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our mission remains the same: make data intelligence accessible to every team, everywhere.
